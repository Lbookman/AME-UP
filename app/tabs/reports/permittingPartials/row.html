Some or all of this land is administered by the and {{owner}}, and therefore all associated electric utility infrastructure is required to obtain a right-of- way (ROW) easement:
  • For more information about obtaining a ROW on lands administered by the BLM, please see: https://www.blm.gov/programs/lands-and-realty/right-of-way/obtaining-right-of-way
  • For more information about obtaining a ROW on lands administered by the USFS, please see https://www.fs.fed.us/dirindexhome/field/r3/fsm/2700/2730.doc
  • For more information about obtaining a ROW on Tribal lands or lands administered by the BIA, please see https://www.bia.gov/as-ia/raca/rights-way-25-cfr-169
  • For more information about obtaining a ROW on lands administered by the Arizona State Land Department, please see: https://land.az.gov/applications-and-permits
  • For more information about obtaining a ROW on lands administered by counties or cities, please contact the appropriate county and/or city planning department.
