Account types
Users can register as a member of one of the following groups.
Energy Developer
Prospective renewable energy developers in the state of Arizona. There are no requirements for this account type.
Military planner
Active service members belonging to a branch of the United States military (Air Force, Army, Coast Guard, Marine Corps, Navy, or National Guard) using the tool as part of regular work duties. Must have a valid .mil email address.
Government agency
Users belonging to a local, state, or federal government agency, and using the tool as part of their regular work duties. Must have a valid email address associated with a verifiable government agency.
Administrator
Account for website administrators.
Other
Members of the public that do not fit into any of the above categories are also permitted to use this tool. There are no requirements for this account type.
